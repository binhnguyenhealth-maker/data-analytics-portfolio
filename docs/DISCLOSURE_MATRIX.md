## Refresh contract

- Synthetic data is regenerated by `shared/validation/regenerate_all.py`.
- The contract is local, deterministic, and versioned (per-project
  `snapshot_id`). Regeneration is byte-identical across fresh interpreter
  processes under distinct `PYTHONHASHSEED` values (enforced by a
  subprocess-level determinism test under at least three seeds).
- A real refresh contract (scheduled acquisition, source reconciliation,
  exception handling) would be added for a production deployment and is out
  of scope here.

## Boundary with other work (important)

This repository is a **standalone synthetic portfolio prototype**. It
is deliberately and completely separate from any other work the author may
have performed, including any client, production, or third-party engagement.
In particular:

- This portfolio is **not** derived from, and claims no relationship to, any
  client deliverable, solicitation response, procurement engagement, or
  production deployment performed by the author or any other party. Any
  same-named analytical pattern elsewhere is coincidental; this tree's data,
  identifiers, and receipts are synthetic and self-contained.
- No output here is evidence of paid delivery, client acceptance, production
  deployment, hosted analytics administration, security implementation,
  training delivery, maintenance history, or references for any real
  engagement. Those require separate evidence that is intentionally absent
  from this tree.
- The disclosure scan (`shared/validation/scan.py`) fails closed on
  organization names, client identifiers, private paths, access material,
  and unsupported production/client claims appearing anywhere in tracked
  content.

## Native Tableau boundary

- Native Tableau authoring and final reopen checks were completed in Tableau
  Desktop 2026.2 Free Edition.
- Each packaged workbook contains only generated CSV inputs from this
  repository and uses relative package paths.
- The structure and package validators fail closed on any extra workbook,
  preview, embedded input, absolute local path, or unapproved binary.
- Optimizer was visible but disabled in the Free Edition; no hosted-platform
  capability is claimed.
